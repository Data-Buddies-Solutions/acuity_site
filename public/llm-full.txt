# Acuity Health - AI Receptionist Knowledge Base

## Company Overview
Acuity Health builds an AI receptionist for ophthalmology and optometry practices. The product answers inbound patient calls, books appointments directly into the EMR, captures after-hours demand, and gives the front desk structured follow-up instead of voicemail backlogs.

## Product

### AI Receptionist
- Answers inbound patient calls
- Handles routine scheduling conversations
- Gathers the right patient and visit context
- Supports multilingual conversations, including Spanish
- Routes medical vs. vision insurance workflows
- Supports pediatric and urgent-visit routing
- Escalates to staff when human help is needed

### EMR Booking
- Writes appointments directly into the practice EMR
- Reads provider, location, visit type, and scheduling rules
- Reduces manual callbacks and double entry
- AdvancedMD integration is available today
- Athena and Compulink integrations are in progress

### After-Hours Capture
- Answers calls when the front desk is closed
- Converts after-hours demand into booked appointments when rules allow
- Sends confirmations and follow-up context
- Reduces voicemail leakage and next-day callback load

### Practice Analytics
- Surfaces call volume, booking outcomes, staff time saved, call handling, and follow-up needs
- Helps multi-location practices understand demand across offices
- Gives operators a clearer view of missed-call risk and front-desk load

## Target Customers
- Ophthalmology practices
- Optometry practices
- Multi-location eye care groups
- Practices with high call volume or high voicemail leakage
- Practices that need EMR-native scheduling
- Practices serving multilingual patient populations
- Practices where staff spend too much time on repetitive phone work

## Problems Acuity Solves
- Patients sent to voicemail during business hours
- After-hours appointment demand that is never captured
- Long hold times and abandoned calls
- Front-desk overload from repetitive scheduling calls
- Manual callback work after missed calls
- Scheduling errors from double entry or disconnected systems
- Inconsistent handling of insurance, pediatric, urgent, and multilingual workflows

## Differentiators
1. **Ophthalmology focus** - Built around eye care scheduling, insurance, routing, and urgency patterns.
2. **Direct EMR booking** - Designed to complete appointments, not just take messages.
3. **High-concurrency call handling** - Answers multiple patient calls at once without hold queues.
4. **After-hours capture** - Converts demand when patients are ready to book.
5. **Human escalation** - Transfers exceptions with context when the call needs staff.
6. **Operational analytics** - Helps practices see call demand, booking outcomes, and front-desk load.

## Integrations
- AdvancedMD: available today
- Athena: in progress
- Compulink: in progress
- Other EMRs: prioritized based on practice demand

## Pricing Model
Pricing is scoped around implementation, practice complexity, location count, workflow depth, and call volume. Implementation covers onboarding, workflow setup, routing logic, integration work, and go-live support. Monthly pricing reflects software, support, reporting, and usage.
