# AI Disclosure - Acuity Health

## Product AI Use
Acuity Health builds an AI receptionist for ophthalmology practices. The product uses voice AI and supporting automation to answer patient calls, gather scheduling context, route requests, book appointments into the practice EMR, send confirmations, and escalate to staff when needed.

Patients are informed when they are speaking with an AI assistant. Human handoff is used for exceptions, clinical escalation, urgent issues, and practice-specific workflows that require staff involvement.

## Content and Development AI Use
The Acuity Health team may use AI tools to assist with content drafting, code development, debugging, and analysis. Human team members review, edit, and approve public-facing content and production code before release.

## Data Practices
Acuity is implemented with HIPAA-conscious workflows and vendor agreements intended to prevent patient data from being used for model training. Practice-specific deployment details, integrations, and data handling are reviewed during implementation.
