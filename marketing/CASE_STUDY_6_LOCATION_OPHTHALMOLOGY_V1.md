## 4. Verification Needed Before Publication

These claims are strong enough to use, but public website copy should define the measurement window.

Confirm:

- whether `0 missed calls` is true over a 30-day period, 90-day period, or current average
- whether `2,000+ after-hours calls answered` is monthly, quarterly, or cumulative to date
- whether `400 staff hours returned` is monthly, quarterly, or cumulative
- whether `500+ appointments booked per month` is average monthly current volume
- whether `100+ concurrent calls handled` is tested capacity, observed peak, or production capability

Recommended public-proof rule:

Whenever possible, use one of these qualifiers:

- `per month`
- `in the first 90 days`
- `during a recent 30-day period`
- `to date`

If those time frames are not yet confirmed, use this line internally:

`Current reported deployment metrics indicate...`
